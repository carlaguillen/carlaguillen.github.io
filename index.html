About

Hello! My name is Carla, nice to meet you!

  • 1988-2005

    My Humble Beginnings

    I was born in Brazil and since I was a child, I've always loved all kinds of animals, and owned many of them, from ordinary dogs to turtles and lobsters.

  • 2005-2012

    Engineering?!

    Yes, I've tried the standard way, graduated as a Computer Engineer in one of the bests universities of Brazil, but after a few years working in a office decided to follow my heart and change my life.

  • 2015

    Panda happened

    I found little Panda abandoned and adopted him. Little did I know that this would change my life entirely. He was stubborn, destroyed my entire house (twice), bit me (yes, more times than I can count). I could have chosen the easy path but decided to give my best to understand him and help our relationship.

  • 2016

    Dog Behaviorist

    I jumped in with both feet in dog behavior world, learning from books and courses. As Panda started to improve and become a better dog, I finally found my passion, I wanted to work with dogs! And was invited to join as a dog behaviorist in a positive training company and had just finished the training and started with a couple of clients when...

  • 2017

    London calling

    My husband was transfered to UK, and here I am, happily reinventing myself once again.
